Bed Regions Used For Concordance

    We defined 4 core bed regions to calculate statistics within, and three additional regions representing shared footprints of some of the core regions:

    • hg38 (entire genome): global bed, chromosomes 1-22 + chrX, chrY, chrM of the hg38 build.
    • giabHC: per-GIAB sample bed, GIAB-defined high-confidence callable regions for short reads.
    • ultima: global bed, Ultima-defined high-confidence regions across the genome.
    • clinvar_genes: global bed, created by selecting all CDS+UTR (+/- 500bp) for genes with ≥1 ClinVar variant. Non-genic regions extracted +/- 500bp if variants did not hit a gene.
    • giabHC_x_ultima: per-GIAB sample bed, shared footprint of giabHC + ultima.
    • giabHC_x_clinvar_genes: per-GIAB sample bed, shared footprint of giabHC + clinvar_genes.
    • giabHC_x_ultima_x_clinvar_genes: per-GIAB sample bed, shared footprint of giabHC + ultima + clinvar_genes.
